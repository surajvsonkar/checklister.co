disorganization

Addressing disorganization typically involves implementing strategies such as creating schedules, prioritizing tasks, setting goals, and maintaining clear communication.

time wastage

Time wastage refers to the inefficient or unproductive use of time, resulting in a lack of accomplishment or progress. It can occur due to various factors such as procrastination, lack of focus, poor time management, or engaging in activities that do not contribute to one's goals or well-being.

Stress

Stress is a physiological and psychological response to external pressures or challenges, often referred to as stressors. It triggers a "fight or flight" reaction in the body, releasing hormones such as cortisol and adrenaline.
